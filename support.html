Frequently asked questions

Does TilawaTrack show the Quran text?

No. TilawaTrack is a companion to whatever you already read from — a physical Mushaf, another app, or your memory. It listens while you recite aloud and counts your words.

Does it record my recitation?

No. The microphone is used only to count words. No audio is saved or sent anywhere. See our Privacy Policy.

The word count isn't working.

Make sure you granted microphone permission (iOS Settings → TilawaTrack → Microphone). Recitation recognition needs the Arabic language; on first use, iOS may take a little time to download the on-device Arabic model, and until then the app uses online recognition (you'll see a note).

Why is my mosque still blurry?

Mosques reveal as your total recited words add up. The reveal happens after each session ends (not while you recite, to keep your focus on worship).

I bought a pack but don't see it.

Go to Settings → Restore purchases. Purchases are tied to your Apple ID.

Is this a subscription?

No. Every pack is a one-time purchase. The Patron option unlocks everything, also one-time.

How do I start over?

Settings → Start over clears your sessions, stats, streaks, and mosque progress. This cannot be undone.
